# SafeAir Pro: Comprehensive Air Quality Monitoring & Eco-Social Platform

## üåç Project Vision
**SafeAir Pro** is an advanced ecosystem designed to empower citizens of Kazakhstan to monitor, report, and improve air quality. Beyond simple monitoring, it integrates social networking, gamification, and direct interaction with government authorities to foster a healthier urban environment.

## üéØ Key Modules

### 1. Intelligent Pollution Map ("Color Passport" System)
* **Sector-based Analysis:** Divide cities into micro-districts, accounting for population density, schools, and industrial zones.
* **Real-time Source Tracking:** Uses satellite imagery and sensor data to identify pollution sources automatically.
* **Automated Alerts:** Instant notifications to Emergency Services (MCH) and residents when critical pollution levels are detected.

### 2. Eco-Social Network & Gamification
* **Verified Actions:** Users earn points for eco-friendly actions (planting trees, cycling), verified by AI and community voting.
* **District Battles:** Neighborhoods compete for green grants and infrastructure improvements.
* **Eco-Coins & NFTs:** Internal rewards system offering discounts from partners and digital collectibles for major milestones.

### 3. Health & Safety Personalization
* **Vulnerable Group Support:** Tailored advice for pregnant women, children, the elderly, and people with respiratory conditions (asthma/allergies).
* **Clean Routes:** Navigation system that calculates the healthiest path, avoiding high-pollution zones and utilizing green belts.

### 4. Civic Engagement & Education
* **Direct Reporting:** Multi-step reporting system (Photo/Video/Geo-tag) sent directly to Akimats and ecological police.
* **Learning Platform:** Tiered educational courses (Basics to Expert) with digital certification.

## üõ† Technical Architecture
* **Frontend:** React 19, TypeScript, Vite.
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with a custom-built icon system.
* **UI Components:** shadcn/ui for accessible and professional interface.
* **State Management & Persistence:** LocalStorage for user preferences and i18n for English, Kazakh, and Russian localization.
* **Data Reliability:** Integration with Kazhydromet, WHO, and international research databases.
